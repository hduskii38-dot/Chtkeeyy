The one rule, and a few ground rules

The one rule is: be someone your neighbours would be glad to talk to. Everything below just protects that.

  • No harassment, hate speech, or targeting anyone for who they are.
  • Nothing sexual, and nothing involving minors — Chtkeey is 18+.
  • No sharing anyone's personal details, yours or theirs.
  • Report anything that crosses a line — three reports and someone's out.
  • Chats aren't stored once they end. Screenshotting someone else's words to share elsewhere isn't in the spirit of the room.
